A. Determinan Ekspansi Baris

  1. A = $\(\begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix}\)$

    jawaban :

    Ekspansi baris pertama: $\(det(A)=(−1)1+1(−7)⋅M11​+(−1)1+2(−5)⋅M12​\)$

    • \(M11 = 4\)

    • \(M12 = 1\)

    \[\begin{split} =(+1)(−7)(4)+(−1)(−5)(1)\\ =−28+5=−23 \end{split}\]
  1. \[\begin{split}A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

    jawaban:

    Ekspansi baris ke-3 :

    \[ \sum_{k=1}^3 (-1)^{3+k} a_{3k} {M_3k} \]

    elemen baris ke3 : 0, 0, 1

    Hitung ekspansi : $\(det(A)=(−1)3+1(0)⋅M31​+(−1)3+2(0)⋅M32​+(−1)3+3(1)⋅M33​\)\( \)\( (−1)4(0)(M31​)=0 \\ (−1)5(0)(M32​)=0 \\ (−1)6(1)(M33)=(+1)(1)⋅M33 ​​\)$

    \[\begin{split} M_{33} = \begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2 \\ 1 & -2 \end{vmatrix} = (0)(-2) - (2)(1) = 0 - 2 = -2 \end{split}\]

    Hasil akhir : $\(det(A)=0+0+(1)(−2)=−2​\)\( \)\(det(A)=−2\)$

  1. \[\begin{split}A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix}.\end{split}\]

    jawaban :

    Ekspansi baris ke-4 : $\( \sum_{k=1}^4 (-1)^{4+k} a_{4k} {M_4k} \)$

    elemen baris ke-4 : 1, 1, 1, -3

    Hitung ekspansi : $\(det(A)=(−1)5(1)M41​+(−1)6(1)M42​+(−1)7(1)M43​+(−1)8(−3)M44\)$

    Hitung minor : \(M_{41}\)

    \[\begin{split} M_{41} = \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} = -16 \end{split}\]

    \(M_{42}\)

    \[\begin{split} M_{42} = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 & 1 \end{vmatrix} = 16 \end{split}\]

    \(M_{43}\)

    \[\begin{split} M_{43} = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} = -16 \end{split}\]

    \(M_{44}\) $\( M_{44} = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} = -16 \)$

    Substitusi : $$ \det(A) = (-1)(1)(-16)

    • (1)(1)(16)

    • (-1)(1)(-16)

    • (1)(-3)(-16) $$

    Hasil:

    \[\begin{split} \det(A) = 16 + 16 + 16 + 48 = 96\\ \boxed{\det(A) = 96} \end{split}\]

B. Matriks Adjoin

  1. \[\begin{split}A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

    jawaban :

    Rumus : $\((\operatorname{adj} A)_{ij} = (-1)^{i+j} M_{ji}\\ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det A} \operatorname{adj} A.\)$

    • Determinan

    \[ \det(A) = (-7)(4) - (-5)(1) = -28 + 5 = -23 \]

    • Matriks Kofaktor

    Gunakan tanda: $$ \begin{bmatrix}

    • & - \

    • & + \end{bmatrix} $$

    Minor:

    \[ M_{11} = 4,\quad M_{12} = 1,\quad M_{21} = -5,\quad M_{22} = -7 \]

    Kofaktor:

    \[\begin{split} C = \begin{bmatrix} (+1)(4) & (-1)(1) \\ (-1)(-5) & (+1)(-7) \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & -1 \\ 5 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

    • Adjoin (transpose kofaktor)

    \[\begin{split} \text{adj}(A) = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

    • Invers Matriks

      Gunakan rumus:

    \[ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det(A)} \cdot \text{adj}(A) \]
    \[\begin{split} A^{-1} = \frac{1}{-23} \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

  • Hasil Akhir

\[\begin{split} A^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} -\frac{4}{23} & -\frac{5}{23} \\ \frac{1}{23} & \frac{7}{23} \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
