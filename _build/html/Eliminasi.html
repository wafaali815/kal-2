ELIMINASI GAUSSIAN#

Contoh Penyelesaian Sistem Persamaan Linier Tiga Variabel & Empat Variabel Dengan Menggunakan Eliminasi Gaussian.#

A. Sistem Persamaan Linear Tiga Vriabel

1. Sistem Persamaan

\[\begin{split} \begin{cases} x + y + z = 6 \\ 2x + y - z = 3 \\ x - y + z = 2 \end{cases} \end{split}\]
A = matrix([[1, 1, 1, 6],[2, -1, 1, 3],[1, -1, 1, 2]]) A.add_multiple_of_row(1, 0, -2) A.add_multiple_of_row(2, 0, -1) print (A)

2. Matriks Augmented

\[\begin{split} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1 & | & 6 \\ 2 & 1 & -1 & | & 3 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 & | & 2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

3. Eliminasi Gauss (Manual)

Langkah 1

Membuat semua elemen di bawah pivot pertama menjadi 0

Operasi:

\( R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_1 \)

\( R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - R_1 \)

\[\begin{split} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1 & | & 6 \\ 0 & -1 & -3 & | & -9 \\ 0 & -2 & 0 & | & -4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Langkah 2

\( R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + 2R_2 \)

\[\begin{split} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1 & | & 6 \\ 0 & -1 & -3 & | & -9 \\ 0 & 0 & 6 & | & 14 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

4. Substitusi Balik

\[\begin{split} \begin{aligned} 6z &= 14 \Rightarrow z = \frac{7}{3} \\ -y - 3z &= -9 \Rightarrow y = 2 \\ x + y + z &= 6 \Rightarrow x = \frac{5}{3} \end{aligned} \end{split}\]

5. Solusi

Jadi, nilai x, y, dan z adalah:

\[ x = \frac{3}{5}, \quad y = 2, \quad z = \frac{3}{7} \]

B. Sistem Persamaan Linear Empat Variabel.

Soal

\[\begin{split} \begin{cases} x + y + z + w = 10 \\ 2x + y - z + w = 8 \\ x - y + 2z - w = 3 \\ 3x + 2y + z + w = 14 \end{cases} \end{split}\]
A = matrix([[1, 1, 1, 1, 10],[2, 1, -1, 1, 8],[1, -1, 2, -1, 3],[3, 2, 1, 1, 14]]) A.add_multiple_of_row(1, 0, -2) A.add_multiple_of_row(2, 0, -1) print (A)

1️⃣ Bentuk Matriks Augmented

\[\begin{split} \left[ \begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 10 \\ 2 & 1 & -1 & 1 & 8 \\ 1 & -1 & 2 & -1 & 3 \\ 3 & 2 & 1 & 1 & 14 \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

2️⃣ Eliminasi Gauss

Langkah 1: Hilangkan elemen di bawah pivot pertama (kolom 1)

\( R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_1 \)

\( R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - R_1 \)

\( R_4 \leftarrow R_4 - 3R_1 \)

Hasil:

\[\begin{split} \left[ \begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 10 \\ 0 & -1 & -3 & -1 & -12 \\ 0 & -2 & 1 & -2 & -7 \\ 0 & -1 & -2 & -2 & -16 \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

Langkah 2: Hilangkan elemen di bawah pivot kedua

\( R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + 2R_2 \)

\( R_4 \leftarrow R_4 + R_2 \)

Hasil:

\[\begin{split} \left[ \begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 10 \\ 0 & -1 & -3 & -1 & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 7 & 0 & 17 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 & -4 \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

Langkah 4: Hilangkan elemen di bawah pivot ketiga

R4 ​← R4 ​− 7/1​R3​

Hasil akhir bentuk segitiga atas:

\[\begin{split} \left[ \begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 10 \\ 0 & 1 & 3 & 1 & 12 \\ 0 & 0 & 7 & 0 & 17 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & -1 & -45/7 \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

3️⃣ Substitusi Balik

Dari baris ke-4:

\[ -w = -45/7 \]
\[ w = 45/7 \]

Baris ke-3:

\[ 7z = 17 \]
\[ z = \frac{17}{7} \]

Baris ke-2:

\[ y + 3z + w = 12 \]

Substitusi:

\[ y + 3\left(\frac{17}{7}\right) + \frac{45}{7} = 12 \]
\[ y + \frac{51}{7} + \frac{45}{7} = 12 \]
\[ y + \frac{96}{7} = 12 \]
\[ y = 12 - \frac{96}{7} \]
\[ y = \frac{84}{7} - \frac{96}{7} \]
\[ y = -\frac{12}{7} \]

Baris pertama:

\[ x + y + z + w = 10 \]

Substitusi:

\[ x - \frac{12}{7} + \frac{17}{7} + \frac{45}{7} = 10 \]
\[ x + \frac{50}{7} = 10 \]
\[ x = 10 - \frac{50}{7} \]
\[ x = \frac{70}{7} - \frac{50}{7} \]
\[ x = \frac{20}{7} \]

=> Jawaban Akhir

Jadi nilai dari variabel x, y, z, dan w adalah:

\[ x = \frac{20}{7}, \quad y = -\frac{12}{7}, \quad z = \frac{17}{7}, \quad w = \frac{45}{7} \]
