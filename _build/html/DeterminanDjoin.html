matriks

\[\begin{split} [A \mid I] = \left( \begin{array}{cccc|cccc} 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 2 & -1 & 1 & -1 & 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 2 & -1 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 3 & -1 & 2 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \end{split}\]

eliminasi baris

Ubah bagian kiri jadi identitas \(I\):

\[\begin{split} \begin{aligned} \text{Baris } 2 &\to \text{Baris } 2 - 2 \times \text{Baris } 1 \\ \text{Baris } 3 &\to \text{Baris } 3 - \text{Baris } 1 \\ \text{Baris } 4 &\to \text{Baris } 4 - 3 \times \text{Baris } 1 \end{aligned} \end{split}\]

Lanjutkan eliminasi sampai kiri jadi:

\[ [I \mid A^{-1}] \]

Hasil akhir

\[\begin{split} A^{-1} = \begin{pmatrix} -\frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{2} & 0 \\ \frac{3}{2} & -1 & -1 & \frac{1}{2} \\ 1 & -1 & -\frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{2} \\ -1 & 1 & 1 & -\frac{1}{2} \end{pmatrix} \end{split}\]

Tugas Evaluasi KAL2#

A. Hitunglah determinan matrik berikut dengan menggunakan rumus ekspansi baris

\[ \sum_{k=1}^{n} (-1)^{i+k} a_{ik} M_{ik} \]

dengan \(M_{ij}\) adalah minor dari matrik A dan

\[ M_{ij} = \det A_{ij} \]

\(A_{ij}\) adalah submatrik dengan menghapus baris i dan kolom kolom j dari matrik \(A_{nxn}\) dengan \(1 \le i, j \le n\)

\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

B. Gunakan rumus matriks adjoin untuk menghitung invers dari matriks berikut dengan rumus

\[ (\text{adj } A)_{ij} = (-1)^{i+j} M_{ji} \]

dan

\[ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det A} \text{adj } A \]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

JAWABAN

\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\det(A) = a x d - b x c\]
\[ \det(A) = (-7)(4) - (-5)(1) = -28 + 5 = \boxed{-23} \]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

ekspansi baris ke-3 (baris: 0, 0, 1).

Rumus

\[ \det(A) = a_{31}C_{31} + a_{32}C_{32} + a_{33}C_{33} \]

  • $elemen_{31} = 0

  • $elemen_{32} = 0

  • $elemen_{33} = 1

Yang dipakai \(C_{33}\):

\(a_{33}\) (hapus baris 3 kolom 3):

\[\begin{split} \begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2 \\ 1 & -2 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[ (0 \times -2) - (2 \times 1) = 0 - 2 = -2 \]
\[ C_{33} = -2 \]

Hasilnya :

\[ \det(A) = 1 \times (-2) = -2 \]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

markdown

  • Baris 1: \(1 - 3 + 1 + 1 = 0\)

  • Baris 2: \(-3 + 1 + 1 + 1 = 0\)

  • Baris 3: \(1 + 1 - 3 + 1 = 0\)

  • Baris 4: \(1 + 1 + 1 - 3 = 0\)

-> Semua baris jumlahnya 0

markdown \( A \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \)

\[ \det(A) = \boxed{0} \]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

jadi:

\[\begin{split} A^{-1} = \frac{1}{ad - bc} \begin{bmatrix} d & -b \\ -c & a \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

menentukan a, b, c, d

Dari matriks:

  • \(a = -7\)

  • \(b = -5\)

  • \(c = 1\)

  • \(d = 4\)

menghitung determinan

\( \begin{aligned} \det(A) &= ad - bc \\ &= (-7)(4) - (-5)(1) \\ &= -28 + 5 = -23 \end{aligned} \)

Tukar dan mengubah tanda

Gunakan pola: $\( \begin{bmatrix} d & -b \\ -c & a \end{bmatrix} \)$

Hasil: \( \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \)

Kalikan dengan \(\frac{1}{\det(A)}\) $\( A^{-1} = \frac{1}{-23} \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \)$

Hasilnya : \( A^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} -\frac{4}{23} & -\frac{5}{23} \\ \frac{1}{23} & \frac{7}{23} \end{bmatrix} \)

\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Gabungkan dengan matriks identitas:

\[\begin{split} \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|ccc} 0 & 2 & -3 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & -2 & 1 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

  1. Tukar baris

Tukar \(R_1\) dan \(R_2\):

\[\begin{split} \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|ccc} 1 & -2 & 1 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -3 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

Jadikan pivot kolom 2 = 1

Bagi \(R_2\) dengan 2:

\[ R_2 \to \frac{1}{2}R_2 \]
\[\begin{split} \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|ccc} 1 & -2 & 1 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -\frac{3}{2} & \frac{1}{2} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

Hilangkan atas-bawah pivot

Hilangkan kolom 2 di \(R_1\):

\[ R_1 \to R_1 + 2R_2 \]

Hasil:

\[ R_1 = [1 \ 0 \ -2 \ | \ 1 \ 1 \ 0] \]

Hilangkan kolom 3:

  • \(R_1 \to R_1 + 2R_3\)

  • \(R_2 \to R_2 + \frac{3}{2}R_3\)

Hasilnya:

\[\begin{split} \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|ccc} 1 & 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & \frac{1}{2} & 0 & \frac{3}{2} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

bagian kanan \(\to\) hasil invers

\[\begin{split} A^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 2 \\ \frac{1}{2} & 0 & \frac{3}{2} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Rumus:

\[ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det(A)} \cdot \text{adj}(A) \]

Syarat:

\[ \det(A) \neq 0 \]

Hitung determinan

Jumlah tiap baris:

  • Baris 1: \(1 - 3 + 1 + 1 = 0\)

  • Baris 2: \(-3 + 1 + 1 + 1 = 0\)

  • Baris 3: \(1 + 1 - 3 + 1 = 0\)

  • Baris 4: \(1 + 1 + 1 - 3 = 0\)

Semua baris jumlahnya 0

Artinya:

\[\begin{split} A \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Determinan:

\[ \det(A) = 0 \]

Cek invers

\[ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det(A)} \cdot \text{adj}(A) \]

Karena:

\[ hasil \det(A) = 0 \]
